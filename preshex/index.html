Workspace Guide

What is Hexagonal Thinking? It is a learning activity where you find connected conceptual pathways. When edges of two hexagons touch, it implies they share a strong conceptual link!

Basic Controls:

  • Place Hex: Grab a tile from the sidebar deck and slide it onto the field.
  • Grid Snapping: Automatically tiles hexagons with adjacent flat-edges. Toggle it off in the header for completely free placement.
  • Rotate & Annotate: Tap any tile on the field to open options to turn it or write down why you put it there.
  • Pan and Zoom: Drag the background grid to move around. Use the scale buttons to zoom in or out.
