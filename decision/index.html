Scenario #1 - Pizza Toppings (Monarchy)

Your Task:

Your group will select 3-5 toppings representing the perfect combination for pizza lovers everywhere. These toppings must appeal to a broad audience and showcase creativity, flavor balance, and uniqueness.

Steps to Follow:

  1. Select the Monarch: Identify the oldest male student in the group. This person will become the Monarch and take full responsibility for making the final decisions.
  2. Monarch's Role: The Monarch can choose to listen to suggestions from the group but does not have to. The Monarch has the final say.
  3. Group Members' Role: Sit quietly unless the Monarch requests your input. Accept the Monarch's final decision.
  4. Decision Submission: The Monarch shares the final choices and provides a brief explanation for their selections.

Scenario #2 - Best Streaming Service (Direct Democracy)

Your Task:

Your group will assess different streaming services based on their content variety, price, ease of use, and unique features. You must choose the best overall streaming service that offers the best user experience.

Steps to Follow:

  1. Elect a Group Leader: Hold a quick vote to elect a leader to facilitate the discussion and lead the voting process.
  2. Group Discussion: Discuss each service, considering content, price, ease of use, and special features. Everyone must participate.
  3. Voting: The leader will call for votes. Each decision requires a majority vote (more than half the group).
  4. Final Decision: Write down the final choice and an explanation that reflects the group's reasons.

Scenario #3 - Classroom Playlist (Oligarchy)

Your Task:

Select 5-7 artists to be featured on the official Classroom Playlist. The artists you choose should reflect a wide variety of tastes and be appropriate for a school setting.

Steps to Follow:

  1. Identify the Leader: The oldest female in the group will be the leader.
  2. Form the Ruling Class: The leader will choose 3 other group members to form an advisory council.
  3. Ruling Class Discussion: The leader and the 3 advisors will discuss the options and work together to choose the artists.
  4. Making Decisions: The ruling class makes the final decision. The rest of the group must accept the outcome without argument.

Scenario #4 - PTA Movie Night (Dictatorship)

Your Task:

Evaluate the movie options list and choose which will be featured at the PTA Movie Night. Consider factors such as age-appropriateness, entertainment value, and broad appeal.

Steps to Follow:

  1. Identify the Dictator: The youngest male in the group will be the Dictator, with complete authority.
  2. Dictator's Role: The Dictator will review the movie list and decide which movie will be shown. They can ask for input, but it is entirely optional.
  3. Group Members' Role: Remain silent unless the Dictator asks for input. You must accept the Dictator's final decision.
  4. Final Decision: The Dictator will announce their decision and briefly explain why that movie was chosen.

Scenario #5 - Who is the G.O.A.T. (Republic)

Your Task:

Choose one athlete to crown as the Greatest Of All Time (G.O.A.T.). Consider their individual achievements, impact on their sport, legacy, and how they have inspired others.

Steps to Follow:

  1. Elect Representatives: Hold an election to choose five representatives who will make the final decision.
  2. Representatives' Role: The elected representatives will discuss the athletes, debate the pros and cons, and vote to decide the G.O.A.T. A majority (3 of 5) is needed.
  3. Non-Representatives' Role: Observe and submit written petitions to the representatives if you want to suggest points for consideration.
  4. Final Decision: The representatives will announce their majority decision and explain their reasoning.
